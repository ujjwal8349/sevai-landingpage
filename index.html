SevAI is an intelligent platform that connects volunteers with real-world needs. It uses data-driven matching to ensure the right people reach the right place at the right time.

About SevAI

SevAI aims to solve the problem of inefficient resource distribution in communities. By combining structured data, intelligent matching, and real-time analytics, it ensures that help reaches where it is needed the most.
